Introduction

Rahimia Institute of Quranic Sciences strives to foster
collectivism and instill unity of thought among the responsible
youth of the country. With its campuses throughout the country,
Rahimia Institute is rapidly preparing conscientious, intelligent,
socially-aware human resource educated to take on the
challenge of country’s development.

Rahimia Institute considers the youth of this country an asset
which must be valued and groomed to accept the responsibility
of this society. The Institute manages to do this by proactively
engaging the youngsters through various seminars, discussion
forums,Friday congregations and numerous other activities. The
primary objective of all these activities is to instill among
Pakistani youth the significance of systematically understanding
the Quranic principles of a worthy society.

The main campus at Lahore has a fully equipped modern library.
The Institute publishes a research journal “Shaoor-o-Agahi”
along with a monthly “Rahimia Magazine” which features current
affairs analyses and brief descriptions of Quran and Hadith for
contemporary minds. Along with these, the Publications department
of Rahimia Institute, regularly publishes books on important
issues of religious and historical significance.
